## Description

This accession consists of an original metal folding chair used in the WMRN radio studio during the 1940s. The chair shows visible wear consistent with long-term studio use and is believed to have been used by staff and visitors during early broadcast operations.

## Archival Notes

The artifact remains in a controlled private residential environment. Due to age and condition, public handling is restricted. Display requires prior authorization.
