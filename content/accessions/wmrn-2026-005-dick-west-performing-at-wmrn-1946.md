## Description

This accession consists of three scanned black-and-white photographs depicting Richard “Dick” West performing live at WMRN in 1946. Two images clearly display WMRN branding on the microphone. The third image is identified as WMRN-related based on scrapbook labeling (“WMRN 1946”) from which the scan was made. According to donor correspondence dated January 2026, West was approximately 23 years old at the time of the photographs. All images originate from a scrapbook assembled by the donor’s Aunt Barb following West’s death in 1997. Identifications and dates are based on donor recollection and contemporaneous scrapbook annotations and have not yet been independently verified.
