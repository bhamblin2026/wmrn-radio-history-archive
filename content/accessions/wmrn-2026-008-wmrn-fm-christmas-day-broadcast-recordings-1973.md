## Description

This accession consists of off-air recordings of WMRN-FM Christmas Day programming from December 25, 1973. Content includes Christmas music programming, station announcements, commercial advertisements, news segments, and public service announcements.

The recordings were made by the donor using a Teac reel-to-reel recorder and later digitized for archival preservation.

## Archival Notes

The original reel-to-reel tapes remain in private custody. Digitized files are preserved within the WMRN Radio History Archive. Further processing and content indexing remain pending.
