## Description

This accession documents the “American WMRN” microphone flag as it appears in period photographs of WMRN broadcasts and public appearances during the station’s network-affiliated era. Although the original physical microphone flag no longer exists, its design, usage, and historical significance are preserved through surviving photographic documentation.

This accession record formally recognizes and indexes the artifact’s documented presence in WMRN broadcast history.

## Archival Notes

The original microphone flag is considered non-extant. All information associated with this accession is derived from photographic evidence. Rights and reproduction considerations are dependent upon the originating photographic sources.
