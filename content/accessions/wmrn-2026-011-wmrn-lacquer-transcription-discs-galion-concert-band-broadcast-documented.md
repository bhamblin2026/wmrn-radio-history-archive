## Description

This accession documents photographic evidence of lacquer transcription discs and associated labels dated December 18, 1953. The labels list musical selections and playback instructions connected to a Galion Concert Band broadcast.

The original physical lacquer discs no longer survive. All descriptive metadata associated with this accession is derived from visual documentation of the labels.

## Archival Notes

This accession represents documentation-only evidence of broadcast media formerly in existence. The original discs are considered non-extant. Rights and reproduction considerations are tied to the originating photographic sources.
