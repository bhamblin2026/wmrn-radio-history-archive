## Description

This accession consists of a black-and-white photograph depicting entertainer Fred Astaire speaking at a microphone bearing a WMRN station placard during a World War II War Bond fundraising event in Marion, Ohio.

Identification and contextual information are supported by a Facebook post shared by Amy Evers Roston in 2019 and subsequently forwarded to the WMRN Radio History Archive by Randy Winland in January 2026. The accession is cataloged as a single intellectual item with multiple associated digital files.

## Archival Notes

This accession represents a digital reproduction. The location of the original physical photograph remains unknown. Rights research is ongoing to determine appropriate usage and reproduction permissions.
