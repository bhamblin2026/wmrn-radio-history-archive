## Description

This record consolidates all Buckeye Chuck–related images sourced from a Marion Star PDF photo gallery published January 30, 2026, along with additional Marion Star images captured via screenshots. The collection documents Groundhog Day celebrations in Marion, Ohio, from 1989 through 2025, including Buckeye Chuck, public crowds, WMRN Radio and iHeartMedia Marion studios, handlers, community traditions, and institutional partners. All materials are grouped intentionally under a single accession for unified historical context.
