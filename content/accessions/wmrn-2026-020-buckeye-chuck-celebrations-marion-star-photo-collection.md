## Description

This accession consolidates Buckeye Chuck–related images sourced from a Marion Star PDF photo gallery published January 30, 2026, along with additional Marion Star images captured via screenshots. The collection documents Groundhog Day celebrations in Marion, Ohio, from 1989 through 2025.

The images include Buckeye Chuck, public crowds, WMRN Radio and iHeartMedia Marion studios, handlers, community traditions, and institutional partners. All materials are intentionally grouped under a single accession to preserve unified historical context.

This accession consists exclusively of digital reproductions of copyrighted material originally published by The Marion Star. Materials are preserved for research and documentation purposes.
