## Description

This accession consists of an official State of Ohio proclamation issued by Auditor of State Keith Faber on February 2, 2026, recognizing Buckeye Chuck for outstanding community service. The proclamation acknowledges Buckeye Chuck’s designation as Ohio’s official groundhog in 1979 and his role in Groundhog Day celebrations in Marion, Ohio for 47 consecutive years.

The document is associated with WMRN’s long-standing coverage of Buckeye Chuck and Groundhog Day events.
