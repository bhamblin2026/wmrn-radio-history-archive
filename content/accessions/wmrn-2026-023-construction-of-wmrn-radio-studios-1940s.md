## Description

This accession consists of a black-and-white photograph documenting construction of the original WMRN radio studios in Marion, Ohio, during the 1940s. The image shows a partially completed brick structure with exposed roof framing and a radio transmission tower erected adjacent to the building.

Snow-covered ground and horse-drawn wagons are visible, illustrating mid-20th-century construction methods. The photograph represents one of the earliest known visual records of WMRN’s broadcast facilities.
