## Description

Black-and-white photograph documenting the construction of the original WMRN radio studios in Marion, Ohio during the 1940s. The image shows a partially completed brick structure with exposed roof framing and a radio transmission tower already erected adjacent to the building. Snow-covered ground and horse-drawn wagons are visible, illustrating mid-20th-century construction methods. One of the earliest known visual records of WMRN’s broadcast facilities.
