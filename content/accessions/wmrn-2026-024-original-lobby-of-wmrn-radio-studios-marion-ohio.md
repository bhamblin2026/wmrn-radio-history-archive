## Description

This accession consists of a black-and-white photograph depicting the original public lobby of the WMRN radio studios in Marion, Ohio. The image shows a checkered tile floor, visitor seating, framed wall documents, and an American flag positioned near a reception desk.

Through open doors, a larger room with rows of folding chairs is visible, suggesting use for public meetings, broadcasts, or station events. Architectural style and furnishings are consistent with mid-20th-century radio station facilities.

## Archival Notes

This accession is held within the WMRN Radio History Archive Collection. The photograph provides important documentation of the station’s early public-facing architectural environment.
