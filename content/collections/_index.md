# Archival Collections

The WMRN Radio History Archive maintains structured collections documenting the broadcast legacy of WMRN-AM (1490) and its associated stations in Marion, Ohio.

Materials are organized by format, function, and historical context to support research, exploration, and long-term preservation.

---

## Audio Archive

Broadcast recordings, airchecks, interviews, promotional spots, and historic on-air segments documenting the sound of WMRN across decades.
