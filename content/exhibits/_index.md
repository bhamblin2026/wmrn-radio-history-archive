# Digital Exhibits

The WMRN Radio History Archive presents a limited series of curated digital exhibits highlighting major milestones, traditions, and defining moments in the station’s history.

Each exhibit integrates archival materials, documented research, and contextual narrative to present a structured historical record.

---

## Current Exhibits

### Buckeye Chuck & Groundhog Day
A documented history of WMRN’s long-running Groundhog Day broadcast tradition established in 1969 and formally recognized by the Ohio General Assembly in 1979.  
[View Exhibit →](/exhibits/buckeye-chuck/)

---

### WMRN Peanut Push
A historical overview of the community fundraising tradition established in 1969 by broadcaster Charlie Evers in support of the Junior Service Guild’s Christmas Clearinghouse.  
[View Exhibit →](/exhibits/wmrn-peanut-push/)
