# Articles of Incorporation  
## The Marion Broadcasting Company  
Filed April 22, 1940

The Marion Broadcasting Company was formally incorporated in the State of Ohio on April 22, 1940. This filing established the legal corporate entity responsible for constructing and operating radio station WMRN in Marion, Ohio.

The incorporation was recorded in Volume 471, Page 131, in the records of the Ohio Secretary of State.

The company was authorized to issue 250 shares of no par value stock and began operations with a declared capitalization of $15,000.

The incorporators were:

- Robert T. Mason  
- Fred E. Guthery  
- Howard F. Guthery  

---

## Filing Details

• Date Filed: April 22, 1940  
• State: Ohio  
• County: Marion  
• Recorded: Volume 471, Page 131  
• Initial Capitalization: $15,000  
• Authorized Shares: 250 (no par value)
