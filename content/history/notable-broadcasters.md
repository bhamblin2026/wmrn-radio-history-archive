# Notable Broadcasters & Staff

The individuals listed below are documented as having contributed to the on-air identity, newsroom operations, technical infrastructure, and institutional development of WMRN-AM (1490) and its associated FM operations in Marion, Ohio.

Inclusion reflects documented association and historical significance. This list is not exhaustive and will expand as additional records are verified and accessioned.

---

## Broadcasters & On-Air Personalities

### Cole, Terry  
News and production staff member during the 1990s. Contributed to newsroom operations and station production during a period of operational and programming transition.

### Evers, Charlie  
Broadcaster associated with local news and severe weather coverage, including documented service during the Blizzard of 1978.

### James, Paul  
Long-standing on-air presence associated with continuity programming into later operational eras.

### Ruth, Jeff  
Morning show host and on-air personality associated with local programming initiatives during the late twentieth century.

### Shaver, Scott  
Broadcaster active during a period of format and operational evolution.

### Turner, Mark  
On-air talent associated with later-era programming shifts.
